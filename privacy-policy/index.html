Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Welcome to Taglish Translate. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Taglish Translate, we may collect the following:

  • Translation Input: The text you enter for translation. We process this text solely to provide the translation service.
  • Usage Data & Metadata: Including your IP address, browser type, and timestamps of requests. This is used for rate-limiting, security, and service monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide the translation service via third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI).
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our rate limits.
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the website.

3. Third-Party Services

Your translation input is sent to our AI providers (such as OpenAI) in order to generate results. These third-party services are subject to their own data usage policies. We do not use your text inputs to train our own distinct AI models.

4. Data Security

We implement sound technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, processing, or disclosure, in alignment with the guidelines of the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

5. Your Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have rights as a data subject, including the right to be informed, the right to object, the right to access, and the right to rectification or erasure. For inquiries regarding your privacy rights, please contact us.
